About Me

Your Coach, Your Partner

Humayra Farjana Bubly is a Dhaka-based fitness trainer with 8+ years of hands-on gym experience across established fitness centers. She specializes in Yoga, Zumba, Aerobics, strength training, cardio conditioning, and flexibility work.

Her coaching is built around a simple promise: safe technique, motivational support, and personalized training plans that help clients build consistency, confidence, and sustainable fitness habits.

Fitness has been a consistent part of Bubly's lifestyle — reflected in her own daily habits of exercise and yoga. Her coaching personality is hard-working, punctual, patient, and creative.
